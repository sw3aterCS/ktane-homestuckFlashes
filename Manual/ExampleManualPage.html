Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Homestuck Flashes

On the Subject of Homestuck Flashes

[S] Defuse.

  • The module displays a screen with three symbols on it in a specific order.
  • Below the symbols is another screen with names of songs from the webcomic Homestuck. Press the left and right arrow keys to scroll through the list of songs. Press the checkmark to submit the current song.
  • Using the tables below, use the symbols on the module and match them with a specific song. Then, submit that song. If multiple songs are listed in a single entry, use the first one. This module has three stages in total.
